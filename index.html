Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?

Usually, we deliver within 30-45 minutes depending on your location and restaurant preparation time.

Is there a minimum order value?

No! You can order as little or as much as you want. Small order fee may apply for orders under ₹100.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GooglePay, PhonePe), Credit/Debit Cards, Net Banking, and Cash on Delivery.

How can I track my order?

Once you place an order, go to the 'Profile' section or use the live tracking link sent to your phone.
